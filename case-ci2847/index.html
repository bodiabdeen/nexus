Case Overview

On the evening of December 15, 2024, the Heritage City Museum became the scene of an audacious crime. Dr. Victoria Sterling, the museum's esteemed curator, vanished without a trace. That same night, the priceless Midnight Diamond—a 45-carat black diamond valued at $12 million—disappeared from its display case.

The circumstances are deeply suspicious: no forced entry, security systems compromised from the inside, and four individuals with means, motive, and opportunity. Was Dr. Sterling the mastermind, or is she the victim of an elaborate conspiracy?

Your mission: Examine the evidence, interrogate the suspects, and uncover the truth.
