Background

Dr. Elena Voss was the lead scientist on Project Helix, a groundbreaking vaccine platform with potential applications across multiple viral diseases. The project represented 3 years of research and was valued at approximately $50 million in potential pharmaceutical revenue. Dr. Voss held sole authority over the complete formula and had been protective of the intellectual property, refusing to share full documentation even with senior management.

Known for her meticulous nature and demanding leadership style, Dr. Voss had recently become increasingly paranoid about corporate espionage. She had implemented additional security protocols and was in the process of patenting the formula under her own name, causing significant tension with BioGenex executives.

Time of Death

Estimated: 09:47 AM, November 27, 2024

Dr. Voss arrived at her laboratory at 07:15 AM. Security footage shows she remained in Research Wing 3 continuously until her death. She was discovered collapsed at her workstation by research assistant Marcus Chen at 09:52 AM. Emergency services pronounced her dead at 10:08 AM.

Cause of Death

Acute Cyanide Poisoning

Toxicology analysis confirmed lethal levels of potassium cyanide in her system. The poison was administered via her morning coffee, which was found on her desk approximately 75% consumed. Time to death after ingestion: 8-12 minutes based on concentration levels. The coffee mug contained residue consistent with laboratory-grade potassium cyanide from BioGenex's own chemical storage.
