Overview

The following timeline has been reconstructed from multiple witness statements, physical evidence, and documented activities. Each entry represents a verified event during the crucial hours leading to Lord Blackwood's death. Pay particular attention to who had access to the victim, the tonic bottle, and the medicine cabinet during critical time windows.

Critical Timeline Analysis

The timeline reveals a clear window of opportunity and suggests coordination between Lady Catherine and Dr. Helena Ashworth.

Key Observations:

1. Preparation Phase (3:00 PM - 5:25 PM)
- Extended 75-minute private meeting between Catherine and Helena (highly unusual)
- Catherine found crying afterward
- Helena immediately prepares tonic alone for 20-25 minutes
- Helena appeared agitated when returning tonic to cabinet
2. Tonic Administration (8:00 PM)
- Lord Blackwood consumes poisoned tonic at precisely 8:00 PM
- Comments it "smells stronger than usual" but drinks it
- This is approximately 3 hours after Helena prepared it
- Catherine checks at 8:55 PM to confirm Edmund still in study
3. The Suspicious Visit (9:05 PM - 9:33 PM)
- Helena visits Catherine privately 9:05-9:20 PM
- Helena then enters Edmund's study 9:25-9:33 PM
- This is 1 hour 25 minutes AFTER Edmund consumed the poison
- Helena exits flushed and departs immediately without customary pleasantries
- Catherine takes sleeping draught and retires (establishing alibi)
CRITICAL QUESTION: What was Dr. Ashworth doing in Lord Blackwood's study from 9:25-9:33 PM, more than an hour after he consumed the poisoned tonic? Was she checking to see if the poison had taken effect? Was she ensuring he consumed more of the tonic? This visit is highly suspicious and unexplained.
ALIBI ANALYSIS: Lady Catherine established her alibi by taking a sleeping draught at 9:40 PM, witnessed by Agnes Cooper. This places her in bed, sedated, before Lord Blackwood's death. However, the planning phase (afternoon meeting with Helena) and the coordination (Helena's visit at 9:05 PM, then to Edmund at 9:25 PM) suggests conspiracy.
