Inspector Caldwell's investigation notes reveal a crucial pattern:

"This murder required not just one person, but coordination between two individuals with complementary motives."

Cross-reference the Personal Correspondence with the Timeline. Pay very close attention to Document 5 - the note dated October 29th.

From the note:

"I have found the answer we discussed... ensure he takes his tonic as usual at 8:00 PM... By tomorrow evening, your trials will be over."

Now examine the Timeline carefully. Who had an extended private meeting on the afternoon of October 30th? Who prepared the tonic that same day? Who visited whom immediately before departing the estate?

The murder was not committed by one person alone. Look for two individuals who:

  • Had close personal relationship
  • Both had strong motives
  • One had the means (medical knowledge)
  • One had financial motive
  • Their actions on October 30th show coordination

This was a conspiracy. Find the two people working together.
